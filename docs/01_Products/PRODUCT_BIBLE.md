# Executive Summary

Nexora HRMS is a modern, cloud-first, enterprise-ready Human Resource Management System (HRMS) designed to help organizations manage the complete employee lifecycle from recruitment to retirement.

Rather than focusing on a single HR function, Nexora HRMS provides a modular platform that enables organizations to manage recruitment, employee records, attendance, leave, payroll, documents, performance, and more through a unified system.

The platform is designed with scalability, security, and maintainability at its core, making it suitable for organizations of different sizes and industries.

---

# Product Overview

Nexora HRMS is built as a modular business platform where every feature is developed as an independent module while remaining part of one unified ecosystem.

Organizations can configure the system according to their operational needs without modifying the application's core architecture.

The platform follows a cloud-first approach while remaining deployable on customer-managed infrastructure when required.
